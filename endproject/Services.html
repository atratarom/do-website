Overview

The Vehicle Route Tracking System is a software solution designed for transport and fleet companies to monitor the location of various vehicles, including cars, buses, trucks, trailers, and more. By utilizing GPS technology, this system provides drivers and companies with real-time insights into the geographic position of their vehicles.

4. Communication and Reporting Services
